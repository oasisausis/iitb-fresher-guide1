Terms you will see

DX, II, W and AU

DX generally denotes an attendance-related failure. II is incomplete, often connected to an approved missed examination. W indicates withdrawal. AU is audit status.

Academic regulations and deadlines can change. Treat this page as orientation, then verify the current rule in ASC and official institute communication.
