Arrival routes

Get to campus with less drag.

Choose a route based on luggage, arrival time and comfort—not only the lowest fare.

By long-distance train

Railway station → Kanjurmarg → campus

From major terminals, connect to the Central Line where practical and reach Kanjurmarg. From Kanjurmarg West, take an auto-rickshaw to IIT Bombay Main Gate.

With heavy luggage or a late arrival, a direct cab is usually the lower-friction choice.

By flight

Airport → Metro / cab → campus

From either airport terminal, use the current Mumbai Metro route toward the JVLR/Powai side, then take an auto for the final leg. Route names and interchanges can change, so check a live map on arrival.

Before leaving home

Keep your onboarding pass accessible

Store the official joining/onboarding email and its barcode or QR pass offline. Until your permanent ID is ready, it may be needed for first-entry verification at the Main Gate.
