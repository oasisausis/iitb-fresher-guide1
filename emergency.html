Emergency contacts.

For immediate danger anywhere in India, call 112. Inside campus, contact QRT and the relevant campus service as well.

Call 112 now

When calling

Say location first

Give the hostel/building, floor, room or nearest landmark; what happened; number of people at risk; and your callback number.
